Welcome

A calmer kind of grooming salon.

Welcome to Hampton Hounds — professional dog and cat groomers in the heart of Hampton Village. We offer a considered selection of pet services from our salon, delivered with patience and genuine care.

We mainly serve South West London & Surrey — though we're proud to say some of our clients travel from as far as Brighton and even Manchester to see us.

"We look forward to welcoming you and your pet to our friendly salon soon."

What we offer

Grooming, tailored to your pet

Two signature grooming services, plus specialist cat care — every appointment shaped around your pet's coat, breed and temperament.

01

Full Pet Groom

Our most popular option. Nails, ears, paw pads, sanitary, bath and brush — plus a complete hair cut, styling and finishing cologne.

02

Bath & Brush

Nails, ears, paw pads, sanitary, a thorough bath and brush, plus a light tidy-trim around the head, face and eyes.

03

Cat Grooming

A fast-growing part of what we do. Calm, unhurried sessions with Thiago — our resident cat whisperer — in our dedicated cat room, a quiet space away from the main salon. (We can't promise the rest of the salon will be dog-free, but your cat has its own calm retreat.)

Know exactly the cut you'd like? We know all the classic styles — just ask when booking and we'll do our best to oblige.

Our Salon

No cages. Ever.

Here at Hampton Hounds we don't believe in cages. When not being washed or groomed, your dog is free to socialise with the other dogs or relax on one of our comfy beds.

  • Free to socialise — never crated or confined

The people behind the salon

Meet Thiago

The calm, patient hands behind every Hampton Hounds groom — and every bit as fond of your pet as you are.

Head Groomer, Owner & Cat Whisperer

Thiago Jones

Originally from Brazil, Thiago is a City & Guilds qualified groomer and lifelong animal lover who's turned his passion into what he calls 'the best job in the world'. Experienced across many breeds and fully insured, your precious pets are always in safe hands.

He's earned a quiet reputation as the salon's resident cat whisperer — calm, gentle and patient with even the most nervous of cats.

Thiago is supported by a friendly, experienced team who treat every pet as one of their own.
